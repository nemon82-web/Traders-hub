Pro Tips to Size Up Like a Champ

  • Ease In: Start just outside your comfort zone—push the edge, not the cliff.
  • Risk Smart: Cap your risk at 1% per trade to start. As your gut strengthens, nudge it up—stay chill, though.
  • Lock It Down: Stop losses are non-negotiable. They’re your ticket to bold moves without blowups.
  • Test the Waters: Hit a demo account hard with big sizes. Feel the rush without the risk.
  • Mix It Up: Spread your bets across markets—don’t put all your eggs in one basket.
  • Learn Fast: Review every trade. What worked? Tweak and scale accordingly.
